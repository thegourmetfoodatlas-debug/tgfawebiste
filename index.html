A Legacy That Redefined Dining

From the creators of the iconic Indus Indian Restaurant — winner of Best Restaurant in Queensland — Nitin & Arati Bhogale pioneered Australia’s Designer Concept Menu. Their journey includes:

  • Operating five award-winning restaurants in Brisbane
  • Founding The Gourmet Food Atlas (Noosa) — a two-time Best Business Award winner
  • Hosting Queensland Tourism dignitaries and culinary leaders

This is a story of innovation, passion, and the pursuit of excellence.
